Pasta with Seafood

We all know nothing goes better with pasta than seafood. It's just a combination made in heaven. Here's a simple recipe to honour this mix.

Tuna and Tomato

Ingredients
Ingredient Amount needed
Extra virgin oil 6 tablespoons
Small onion finely 1
Garlic cloves, crushed 2
Tin Italian plum tomatoes, chopped 1-400g (14oz)
Tin tuna in oil, drained and flaked 1-200g (7oz)
Salt, black pepper To taste
Dried Pasta 500g (1lb)
Instructions
  1. Heat oil in a large frying pan.
  2. Add your onions and garlic and cook over medium heat unitl softened for 5 minutes while stirrring constantly.
  3. Add tomatoes and simmer rapidly until just thickened for 10 minutes.
  4. Add tuna and cook until hot through, 3 minutes.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Meanwhile cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water.
  7. Drain. Add pasta to the hot sauce and toss well to coat.

Pasta with Butter and Cheese

Nothing is more mouth-watering than cheesy pasta. Check out this flavourful recipe that will bring your taste buds to life.

Butter and Parmesan

Ingredients

Additional freshly grated parmesan, to serve

Ingredient Amount needed
Dried Pasta 500g (1lb)
Butter, cut into cubes 125g (4oz)
Freshly grated parmesan 100g (3 1/2oz)
Salt, black pepper To taste
Instructions
  1. Cook pasta ina large pot of boiling, salted water. Drain. Return pasta to warm pasta pot.
  2. Add butter to pasta and toss well to coat.
  3. Add parmesan and salt and pepper to taste.

    4. Serve immediately with additional parmesan.

Pasta with Meat

Always save the best for the last right? Well here's a recipe for the king of all protien.

Chicken with Lemon and Mushrooms

Ingredients

Additional 30g (1oz) butter to toss

Ingredient Amount needed
Butter 30g (1oz)
Skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut across into 8-2.5cm (1in) wide strips 2
Brown mushrooms, sliced 250g (8oz)
Garlic cloves, crushed 2
Fresh thyme leaves 1 tablespoon
Lemon juice 2 tablespoons
Salt, black pepper To taste
Dried Pasta 500g (1lb)
Instructions
  1. Melt butter into a large frying pan over meduim heat.
  2. Add chicken strips and cook, stirring freqeuntly, until opaque, 4 minutes.
  3. Add garlic, mushrooms and thyme. Cook, strirring frequently , until mushrooms are soft and chicken is through, 4 minutes.
  4. Add lemon juice and salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Meanwhile, cook pasta in a large pot of boiling, salted water.
  6. Drain. Add pasta to hot sauce with additional butter. Toss well to coat.

    7. Serve immediately and ENJOY.
